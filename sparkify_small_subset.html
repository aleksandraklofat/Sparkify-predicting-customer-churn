Model Selection¶

First, I examine three models without any further tuning in order to see which one is delivering best results as measured by the F1 score. This strategy enables me to test models without loosing too much time with computation.

It is important to bear in mind, that the dataset at hand is not balanced and contains not many churned users. Therefore the F1 score is the metrics of choice.

In the second step I chose the one that performs best on the given dataset and tune it further.

Results and Conclusion¶

Overall, Gradient Boositing scored best with this dataset.

  • The F1 Score is very high at 99% (tuned gradient boosted trees). This shows large overfitting probably originating from the fact, that the test data with churned users is very small. Next step would be to run this model on the whole dataset in the cloud.

  • The most important factors in churn seem to be: number of songs played per user, user engagement represented by the thumbs up/down feature and overall listening time.
